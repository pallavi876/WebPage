Globalization has brought in a clear shift from education as transmission of expert knowledge to education as building learner competencies with human values. Engineering education in India is experiencing shift from traditional teaching–learning method to outcome based education. Our department is also looking forward to enhance the quality and make the students globally competitive.

Our educational initiative is to create an academic foundation for technological development in our college which can be extended to global interface by giving way to education exchange in international territory as well. We identify individual talents, nurture them as talented engineers and create world class professionals.

Each course of the department has its learning outcomes that are derived after consultation with all stakeholders, benchmarking and intensive internal discussions. We are striving to collaborate with various industries/institutes of eminence around the globe, which helps student capture opportunities available for them. At present our department has academic collaboration with IITB-Spoken Tutorial, IIRS-Edusat Outreach Program, Virtual Labs and Gauss & Reimann along with this we have industry collaborations with TechMahindra –Makers Lab, Rucha industries, Endress+Hauser, Geneka Technologies.

We are confident that these collaborations will offer students an opportunity to view the world, and experience the cultural and academic diversity that exist globally and will emerge as valuable assets to society.

The vision of department is to develop computer engineers with necessary analytical ability and human values who can implement a wide spectrum of computer system for the welfare of society. In pursuit of this vision, the department actively organizes seminars, workshops, short-term training programs and value added courses providing various opportunities to showcase talents and innovative skills in variety of fields.

I, on behalf of CSE department invite you to learn more about the innovative research and learning process at CSE in MGMU JNEC, Aurangabad.

Dr. Vijaya B. Musande
